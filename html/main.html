Avoided Emission Concept

The avoided emission concept quantifies and rewards the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions by adopting sustainable practices. It involves establishing a baseline, transitioning to eco-friendly alternatives, measuring emissions reduction, quantifying avoided emissions, and converting them into valuable carbon credits. Here are the steps:

  • Establish Baseline: Determine the emissions generated by conventional practices.
  • Transition to Sustainable Practices: Adopt environmentally friendly alternatives.
  • Emissions Reduction: Reduce emissions below the baseline through sustainable practices.
  • Quantify Reduction: Measure and verify the emissions reduction.
  • Convert to Carbon Credits: Transform avoided emissions into tradable carbon credits.
  • Incentivize Sustainability: Encourage the adoption of eco-friendly practices and technologies with financially rewards
